Why This Project?

Nowadays in the digital age, there's a general struggle that so many people face: how do you know if your crush is flirting back to you? This simple question caused so much headache for many man and woman around the world. In the past, there's no way to work through this without an expert wingman by your side. But as you can see, introducing a wingman into the relationship is a risky thing to do. Many cases of wingman taken your crush happen daily.

Today, with modern development in A.I. and Natural Language Processing, I am happy to introduce you to the latest and greatest application of A.I. in semantic understanding. With this tool, you don't need a physical wingman anymore. This alpha version is the start to a brighter future.
